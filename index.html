My Journey..

The question I always get asked is "why get into tech when you have a Sociology degree?" Here's why..

I was introduced to tech when I was trying to figure out what I wanted to do with my life post-university. I attended a webinar about “getting into tech” and heard about the tons of different avenues that form the industry and I was completely sold as it opened my eyes even more to how much technology is shaping the world we live in and I knew I wanted to be a part of it. I had made up my mind that I would one day become a DevOps Engineer as it is a role that requires specialisation in many aspects which excites me, but I knew it was going to be challenging as I had ZERO experience. Working full-time, equipped with thousands of free online resources and a huge amount of resilience, I dedicated my free time to learning how to code in HTML and CSS, attended webinars about various coding languages and tried to build my knowledge so that I could eventually get into the industry.
I became more and more drawn to Cloud Computing and found the AWS re/Start programme in which i was offered a place on the course. It has been the steepest learning-curve I have experienced but in 12 weeks, I have learnt what it would take most people months/years to learn. I now have foundational knowledge in Linux bash scripting, IT Networking and Security, MySQL, Python and AWS.
